<h2>Dichelostemma spp.</h2>

Distinguishing features:
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.virtualherbarium.org/glossary/glossary.php?searchfor=crown&searchin=def&B1=Search">crown</a> color, leaning in or out</li>
<li>perianth tube narrowed above ovary or not</li>
</ul>

The defining difference is the number of stamens, but I can't count them without taking apart the flower.
There is supposed to be a difference in bracts color, but I can't consistently see it on CalPhotos.

{blue dicks-.jpg} {blue dicks}
<ul>
<li>perianth blue, blue-purple, pink-purple, or white</li>
<li><a href="http://www.virtualherbarium.org/glossary/glossary.php?searchfor=crown&searchin=def&B1=Search">crown</a> erect, leaning toward anthers, outward at tip, white</li>
<li>perianth tube not narrowed above ovary</li>
<li>bracts generally dark purple (paler, striped dark purple</li>
<li>common throughout the bay area</li>
</ul>

{ookow-.jpg} {ookow}
<ul>
<li>perianth blue-purple</li>
<li><a href="http://www.virtualherbarium.org/glossary/glossary.php?searchfor=crown&searchin=def&B1=Search">crown</a> erect, leaning away from anthers, +- purple</li>
<li>perianth tube narrowed above ovary or +- so</li>
<li>bracts pale purple to green</li>
<li>scattered throughout the bay area</li>
</ul>
