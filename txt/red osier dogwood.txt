==american dogwood:C. sericea ssp. sericea
. leaf generally dense-rough-hairy abaxially
. petals 3--4.5 mm
. stone generally 3-ridged on faces

==western dogwood:C. sericea ssp. occidentalis
. leaf generally +- glabrous to strigose abaxially
. petals 2--3 mm
. stone generally smooth on faces
