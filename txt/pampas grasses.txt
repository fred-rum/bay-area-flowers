==pampas grass:C. selloana
color: other
. panicle at most only slightly elevated above foliage
. inflorescence stalk 2--3 &times; inflorescence length
. fresh flowers are white/cream (becoming tan with age)
. sheath glabrous or sparsely hairy [<a href="https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0175+3301+2288+0099">example</a>]

==purple pampas grass:C. jubata
color: other
. panicle well-elevated above foliage
. inflorescence stalk 4--5 &times; inflorescence length
. fresh flowers are pale purple (becoming tan with age)
. sheath densely hairy [<a href="https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0108+1022">example</a>]
