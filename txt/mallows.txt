<h2>Malva spp.</h2>

{+bull mallow}
{-
petals 5&ndash;12 mm; sepals 4&ndash;6 mm; petals &plusmn; > or >> sepals
petal veins generally dark
bractlets wider than linear, bases fused to each other or to sepals
-}

{dwarf mallow:Malva neglecta}
{-
petals generally twice as long as sepals; sepals 4&ndash;6 mm
petal veins not dark
bractlets widely linear to narrowly lanceolate, bases free from each other and from sepals
-}

{cheeseweed mallow:Malva parviflora}
{-
petals 3&ndash;5 mm; sepals ~3 mm
bractlets 1&ndash;2 mm long, linear to thread-like
-}

{common mallow:Malva sylvestris}
{-
petals generally 15&ndash;30 mm; sepals 6&ndash;8 mm
petal veins dark
bractlets 3&ndash;7 mm long, 2&ndash;4 mm wide, free
-}

See also {checkerblooms and checkermallows}.
