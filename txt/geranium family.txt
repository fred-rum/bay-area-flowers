==stork's-bills:Erodium spp.
. [typically?] 3 or more flowers per inflorescence (some may be unopened)
. generally 5 fertile stamens alternating with 5 staminodes
. leaf simple or palmately divided; leaf veins pinnate

==geraniums and cranesbills:Geranium spp.
. 2 (rarely 1) flowers per inflorescence (often 1 is unopened)
. generally 10 fertile stamens (5 outer and 5 inner)
. leaf palmately lobed or divided
