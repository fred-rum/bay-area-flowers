Key features:
. bark smooth, shedding, and light colored; or deeply furrowed and dark colored
. leaf shape
. inflorescence shape
. flowers/fruit fused at base or free
. hypanthium shape

==tasmanian blue gum:E. globulus
. individual flowers in leaf axils, sessile
.. [the leaf may be not present]
. fruit glaucous, > 1.5 cm wide
.. all others have fruit not glaucous, < 1 cm wide
. <b>very common</b>

==silver dollar gum:E. polyanthemos
. inflorescence a panicle of umbles
. rare

[
<h2>umbel inflorescence</h2>

==red ironbark:E. sideroxylon
. <b>bark deeply furrowed, dark brown to +- black</b>
.. all below plants have smooth, shedding bark (sometimes rough near base), lighter colored
. inflorescence a 5--7-flowered umbel
. rare

==manna gum:E. viminalis
. inflorescence a <b>3-flowered umbel</b>
. uncommon

==white peppermint:E. pulchella
. <b>leaves linear, generally < 0.5 cm wide</b>
. inflorescence a 9--15-flowered umbel
. <b>fruit valves not exserted</b>
. very rare

==river redbum:E. camaldulensis
. leaves lanceolate, generally > 1 cm wide
. inflorescence a 7--11-flowered umbel
. fruit valves exserted
. <b>bud cap hemispheric, beaked, +- = hypanthium</b> (<a href="http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=25257">figure of bud with cap</a>)
. rare

==forest red gum:E. tereticornis
. leaves lanceolate, generally > 1 cm wide
. inflorescence a 7--11-flowered umbel
. fruit valves exserted
. <b>bud cap horn-shaped to conic, not beaked, +- 2 &times; hypanthium</b>
. very rare

]
