==sargent's cypress:H. sargentii
sci_i:Cupressus sargentii
sci_p:Hesperocyparis sargentii|Cupressus sargentii|Callitropsis sargentii
. crown symmetric, often dense, generally pyramidal in age
. <b>foliage dull, dusty to gray-green, glaucous or not</b>
. very rare

==gowen cypress:H. goveniana
sci_i:Cupressus goveniana
sci_p:Hesperocyparis goveniana|Cupressus goveniana|Callitropsis goveniana
. crown symmetric, often dense, generally pyramidal in age
. foliage bright to dark green, not glaucous
. plant 5--7 m
. generally found on the Monterey Peninsula, much rarer in the Santa Cruz mountains

. foliage bright to dark green, not glaucous
