sci:Amsinckia menziesii

==rancher's fiddleneck:A. menziesii var. intermedia
. corolla radial, tube straight, limb with 0 or 5 dark spots
. stamens generally attached above middle of corolla tube, if below then style exserted
. stem ascending to erect
. corolla 7--11 mm, <b>limb 4--10 mm diam</b>, +- orange, tube exserted
. very common

==small-flowered fiddleneck:A. menziesii var. menziesii
. corolla radial, tube straight, limb with 0 or 5 dark spots
. stamens generally attached above middle of corolla tube, if below then style exserted
. stem decumbent to +- ascending
. corolla 4--7 mm, <b>limb 1--3 mm diam</b>, yellow to orange-yellow, tube +- exserted
. scattered
. also called A. retrorsa
