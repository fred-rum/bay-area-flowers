<h2>Ribes spp.</h2>

Distinguishing features:
<ul>
<li>spines on twig nodes or not</li>
<li>sepal color, spread angle, and number</li>
<li>styles hairy at base or glabrous</li>
<li>stamen length</li>
<li>leaf glandular on underside or not</li>
<li>bristles between nodes on twigs or not</li>
</ul>

I've made very few currant/gooseberry observations, and my identifications are not firm.
