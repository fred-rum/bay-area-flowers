{+davy's centaury}
{-
Leaf: cauline, generally 8--25 mm, elliptic-oblong to ovate
Inflorescence: open; pedicels 2--25(55) mm
Flower: <b>calyx lobes keeled</b>; corolla 12--17 mm, lobes 3--7 mm, ovate-oblong to elliptic, tip rounded
-}

{+muehlenberg's centaury}
{-
Leaf: cauline, 15--25 mm, lower obovate to oblong, upper lance-elliptic
Inflorescence: open; pedicels 0--10 mm
Flower: <b>calyx lobes not keeled</b>; corolla 12--19 mm, lobes 2--7 mm, lance-oblong, tip rounded
-}
