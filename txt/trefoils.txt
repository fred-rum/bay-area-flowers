{sci:n/a}
These are all called "trefoil", but they're not closely related and don't always look particularly similar.  For flowers that look similar to each other, see {-small yellow peas}.

{+bird's-foot-trefoils}
{-
whorl or partial whorl of 1--7 flowers
5 leaflets per leaf in odd-pinnate arrangement
-}

{+chile trefoil}
{-
1 flower per leaf axil
3--9 leaflets, generally irregularly arranged
See also the other short, yellow {-deervetch} species.
-}

{+hop trefoils}
{-
inflorescence <b>head-like or +- spike-like</b>
-}
