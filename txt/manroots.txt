Key features:
{-
corolla cup shaped or not, especially when viewed from the side/underneath
-}

{+california manroot}
{-
corolla <b>rotate</b>, yellow-green to cream or (especially inland) white
<b>[the corolla may have some bowl shape, but it won't show a deep cup when seen from underneath]</b>
fruit 4--5 cm, +- spheric; prickles sparse to +- dense, < 12 mm, +- stiff, unhooked
Ecology: Streamsides, washes, shrubby open areas
-}

{+coastal manroot}
{-
corolla generally > 8 mm wide, <b>deeply cup-shaped</b>, white
<b>[the corolla has a deep cup when seen from underneath]</b>
fruit 4--8 cm, +- spheric, <b>tapered to beak, generally striped dark green</b>; prickles generally dense, stiff or flexible, generally 0 at tip, unhooked
-}
