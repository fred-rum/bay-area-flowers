sci:Maianthemum spp.
x:ca

Key features:
. 4 or 6 perianth parts
. perianth > stamens or << stamens
. leaves with or without petioles

==false solomon's seal:M. racemosum
color: white
. Perianth parts 6
. Inflorescence is a panicle
. > 20 flowers per inflorescence
. <b>perianth 1--2 mm, << stamens</b>
. <b>fruit tan with red speckles</b>
. <b>&gt; 5 leaves</b>, 7--20 cm, ovate to oblong-elliptic; <b>petioles +- 0</b>
. common

==star-flowered lily-of-the-valley:M. stellatum
color: white
. Perianth parts 6
. Inflorescence is a raceme (or few-branched near base)
. 5--15 flowers per inflorescence
. <b>perianth 4--7 mm, &gt; stamens</b>
. <b>fruit tan with red speckles and <a href="https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0210+1472">bold dark purple stripes</a></b>
. <b>&gt; 5 leaves</b>, 5--17 cm, (ob)lanceolate to elliptic; <b>petioles 0</b>
. common

==western lily of the valley:M. dilatatum
. <b>Perianth parts 4</b>
. Inflorescence is a raceme
. 10--50 flowers per inflorescence
. <b>2--3 leaves</b>, 5--20 cm, cordate to sagittate; <b>petioles 5--15 cm</b>
. San Bruno, Marin, Mt. Tam
