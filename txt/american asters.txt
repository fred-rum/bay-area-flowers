==pacific aster:S. chilense
color: pale purple
. stem hairy distally
. outer phyllaries obtuse
. disk flowers: yellow
. ray flowers: 8--12 mm, violet
. very common, especially in the Santa Cruz range

==douglas's aster:S. subspicatum
. stem +- hairy distally
. outer phyllaries +- acute
. disk flowers: yellow
. ray flowers: 8--16 mm, violet
. very rare

==annual saltmarsh aster:S. subulatum
. stem +- glabrous
. disk flowers: yellow
. ray flowers: <b>1.5--3 mm</b>, +- white to pink
. rare
