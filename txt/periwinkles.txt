sci:Vinca spp.

==greater periwinkle:V. major
. corolla 3--5 cm wide
. leaf blade +- 7 cm
. common throughout the bay area

==lesser periwinkle:V. minor
. corolla 2--3 cm wide
. leaf 2--4.5 cm long
. scattered sparsely throughout the bay area, generally near civilization
. not listed in Jepson; data from Wikipedia
