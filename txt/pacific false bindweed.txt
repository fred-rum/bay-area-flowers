sci:Calystegia purpurata
color: white, pale purple

==C. purpurata ssp. purpurata
. stem strongly climbing, > 1 m
. leaf triangular, tip acute, sinus V-shaped
. bracts rarely lobed

==C. purpurata ssp. saxicola
. stem trailing to weakly climbing, generally < 1 m
. leaf ovate-triangular to reniform, tip generally rounded to notched, sinus generally +- closed
. bracts generally with small lobes
