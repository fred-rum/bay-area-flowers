==cabbages and mustards:Brassica spp.
. <b>sepals generally erect to ascending</b>
. basal leaves in a rosette
. distal cauline leaves sessile
. very common

==shortpod mustard:Hirschfeldia incana
. <b>sepals spreading to reflexed</b>
. stigma entire
. <b>basal leaves in a rosette</b>
. cauline leaves dentate to pinnately lobed, petioled or sessile
. very common

==wild mustard:Sinapis arvensis
. <b>sepals spreading</b>
. stigma 2-lobed [?]
. <b>basal leaves not in a rosette</b>
. cauline leaves short-petioled, dentate
. uncommon
