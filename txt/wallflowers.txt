Key features:
. stem base woody or not
. leaves linear or wider, or narrowly linear or narrower
. basal/proximal leaf width, measured exactly
. petal color

==san francisco wallflower:E. franciscanum
color: yellow
. <b>stem base woody</b>; plant subshrub
. scattered on Pacific coast

==western wallflower:E. capitatum
color: orange, yellow
. stem base not woody
. leaves linear or wider, 3--<b>15</b>(30) cm wide
. flower petals <b>orange to yellow</b> (lavender to +- purple)
. Ecology: open areas, alpine, deserts, woodland, <b>sandy areas[?]</b>, chaparral
. scattered

==sand-loving wallflower:E. ammophilum
. stem base not woody
. stem 0.4--9(13) dm
. leaves linear-oblanceolate, 2--9 mm wide
. flower petals yellow
. Ecology: <b>coastal dunes</b>
. rare on Pacific coast near Butano and Big Basin

==santa cruz wallflower:E. teretifolium
. stem base not woody
. leaves 0.3--3 mm wide, <b>thread-like to narrowly linear</b>, margin +- rolled under, appearing cylindric, finely toothed
. Santa Cruz area
