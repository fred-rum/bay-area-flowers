<h2>Sidalcea spp.</h2>

Distinguishing features:
<ul>
<li>thin linear leaves cupping the involucre or not</li>
</ul>

{dwarf checkermallow:dwarf checkermallow-.jpg} {https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+1202+0238:involucre}
{child:dwarf checkermallow}
<ul>
<li>lacks thin linear leaves cupping the involucre; has reduced upper leaves instead</li>
<li>common throughout the bay area</li>
</ul>

{fringed checkerbloom:fringed checkerbloom-.jpg} {https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+1209+0035:involucre}
{child:fringed checkerbloom}
<ul>
<li>has thin linear leaves cupping the involucre</li>
<li>scattered around the bay area</li>
</ul>
