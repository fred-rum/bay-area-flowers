Spotted coralroot has <a href="https://keybase.rbg.vic.gov.au/keys/show/3985?filter_id=55b17b6f8c990">two varieties:</a> <a href="https://www.calflora.org/cgi-bin/species_query.cgi?where-taxon=Corallorhiza+maculata+var.+maculata">var. maculata</a> and <a href="https://www.calflora.org/cgi-bin/species_query.cgi?where-taxon=Corallorhiza+maculata+var.+occidentalis">var. occidentalis</a>.

There are no other coralroot species in the bay area.
