3 petals and 3 sepals look similar to each other<br/>
1 sepal is "upper" and 2 are "lateral"

==broad-leaved helleborine:E. helleborine
. sepals +- green, often purple-tinged or -striped
. lateral petals 8--11 mm; lateral sepals <b>10--13 mm</b>
. +- dry places
. very common in the Santa Cruz range and Oakland hills

==stream orchid:E. gigantea
. sepals +- green to +- red, +- purple-veined
. lateral petals 13--15 mm; lateral sepals <b>16--24 mm</b>
. +- wet places
. scattered
