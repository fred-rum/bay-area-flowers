[
<h3>Yellow</h3>

==yellow starthistle:C. solstitialis
. disk flower corolla <b>13--20 mm</b>
. spines <b>straw colored</b>, 10--25 mm
. stem branched distally or throughout, winged
. common

==tocalote:C. melitensis
. disk flower corolla <b>10--12 mm</b>
. spines proximally <b>+- purple or red</b>, 5--10 mm
. generally 1 stem, distally +- branched, winged
. common

==sicilian star-thistle:C. sulphurea
. disk flower corolla <b>25--30 mm</b>
. spines straw-colored or proximally purple-black, 10--25 mm
. stem simple or openly branched, winged
. Castle Rock

]
[
<h3>Purple</h3>

==purple star-thistle:C. calcitrapa

==spotted knapweed:C. stoebe ssp. micranthos
. Sierra Azul & Mt. Umunhum

==iberian star-thistle:C. iberica
. white to pale purple with purple tips
. rare
