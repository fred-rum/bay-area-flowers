==baby blue eyes:N. menziesii var. menziesii
color: pale blue, pale purple
. <b>corolla blue or pale purple with a nearly white center</b>
.. outer colored section often has darker colored veins
.. inner white section often has even darker dots tracing the path of veins

==white baby blue eyes:N. menziesii var. atomaria
color: white
. <b>corolla white with no darker outer color except for vein color</b>
.. outer section often has colored veins
.. inner section often has even darker dots tracing the path of veins
