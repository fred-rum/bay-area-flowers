==california maidenhair fern:A. jordanii
. frond is 2--3-pinnate [terminal leaflets are palmate]
. terminal leaflets are cut/lobed often < &frac14; way to base, margins converging at 90--180&deg; angle

==black maidenhair fern:A. capillus-veneris
. frond is 2--3-pinnate [final division is palmate]
. terminal leaflets are cut/lobed often > &frac14; to &frac23; way to base, margins converging at 45--90&deg; angle

==five-finger fern:A. aleuticum
. frond is palmate-pinnate [can have more than 5 "fingers"]
