{+salsify}
{-
rays purple
5--11 phyllaries, >= flowers when fully bloomed
pappus [seed parachute] +- brown
-}

{+yellow salsify}
{-
rays pale lemon-yellow
8--13 phyllaries, much exceeding flowers when fully bloomed [but not seed ball]
pappus [seed parachute] +- white
-}

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.
