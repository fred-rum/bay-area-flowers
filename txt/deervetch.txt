<h2>perennial?</h2>

==deerweed:A. glaber
color: yellow
. <b>erect or ascending, woody (but green)</b>, bushy branched, <b>50-200 cm</b>
. leaflets glabrous or finely strigose
. corolla yellow
. very common

==bentham's broom:A. cytisoides
. mat-forming or low-ascending, not woody, 10--80 cm
. leaflets glabrous or finely strigose
. calyx lobes narrow, some or all curved outward or hooked
. corolla 8--10 mm, generally <b>white to +- pink</b> (to brick-red when dry, wings cream), often dark-striate
. Butano, Mt. Montara vicinity

==rush broom:A. junceus
. prostrate or low-ascending, not woody, 8--40 cm
. leaflets glabrous or finely strigose
. calyx lobes wide, straight, not hooked
. corolla yellow
. scattered in the Santa Cruz range

==heermann's lotus:A. heermannii
. prostrate, often mat-forming, 30-100 cm
. <b>leaflets hairy</b>; may be green, gray, or silver
. corolla generally 4--6 mm, yellow to +- red, <b>dark-tipped</b>, wings >= other petals [wings < banner in other species?]
. scattered in the Santa Cruz range

<h3>wings generally +- <= keel</h3>

==short-flower deervetch:A. parviflorus
color: cream, pink
. <b>stem ascending to erect, 5--40 cm</b>
. 3--9 leaflets, lanceolate to obovate, +- equal
. peduncle generally > 1 cm, generally bracted
. 1-flowered per leaf axil
. calyx lobes +- <= tube
. corolla 4--6 mm, pink or salmon, quickly fading, wings +- = keel
. common

==spanish clover:A. americanus var. americanus
. prostrate to erect, 5--60 cm
. <b>leaflets generally 3</b> (upper leaves often simple)
. leaflets lanceolate to obovate, +- equal
. peduncle generally > 1 cm, generally bracted
. flowers 1-flowered per leaf axil
. <b>calyx lobes >> tube</b>
. corolla 5--9 mm, +- white or yellow to pink, wings +- = keel
. common

==coastal bird's-foot trefoil:A. maritimus var. maritimus
. prostrate or ascending, 5--50 cm
. leaflets obovate to +- round, terminal generally largest
. peduncle generally > 1 cm, generally bracted
. generally 2--4-flowered per leaf axil (1st-formed often 1--2-flowered)
. corolla 6--10 mm, bright yellow, wings +- = keel
. Mt. Madonna

==foothill deervetch:A. brachycarpus
. mat-forming to ascending, 5--40 cm
. <b>peduncle << 1 cm, bracts 0</b>
. <b>calyx lobes 1--2 &times; tube</b>
. flowers generally 1 per leaf axil
. corolla 5--9 mm, yellow, reddening in age, wings +- = keel
. scattered

==chile trefoil:A. wrangelianus
color: yellow
<b>Caution: ID uncertain</b>
. generally prostrate, branched at base, 5--30 cm
. <b>peduncle << 1 cm, bracts 0</b>
. <b>calyx lobes +- 0.8--1.2 &times; tube</b>
. flowers generally 1 per leaf axil
. corolla 5--9 mm, yellow, reddening in age, wings +- <= keel
. very common

]

<h3>wings +- >= keel</h3>

==chaparral bird's-foot trefoil:A. grandiflorus var. grandiflorus
. decumbent to erect, 10-40(150) cm
. leaf (including petiole) 10--35 mm, axis not flat; 7--9 leaflets, generally 1--1.5 cm
. inflorescence 3--9-flowered
. <b>corolla 15--25 mm</b>, green-white or yellow, wings +- >= keel
. leaf axis not flat or blade-like
. Mt. Tam

==strigose lotus:A. strigosus
. prostrate, often mat-forming, generally branched from base, 3--50 cm
. leaf axis flat, +- blade-like
. 3--9 leaflets, lanceolate to obovate, +- equal
. inflorescence generally 1--2-flowered
. <b>corolla 5--10 mm</b>, yellow, orange or +- red in age, wings generally > keel
. scattered

]
