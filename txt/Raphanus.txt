==wild radish:R. sativus
. petals pink to purple (or rarely white; check for pale purple veins?)
. basal leaf blade 2--60 cm long, 1--20 cm wide, entire or pinnately lobed or divided
.. 1--12 lateral lobes, dentate

==jointed charlock:R. raphanistrum
. petals pale yellow, +- white in age
. leaf blade 3--15 cm long, 1--5 cm wide, entire or pinnately lobed
.. 2--8 pairs of lateral lobes (in pairs), dentate
