Key features: flower diameter

==chilean sea fig:C. chilensis
color: red purple, pink, yellow
. <b>3--5 cm flower diameter</b>
. rose to magenta petals [but CalPhotos also shows yellow]

==sea fig:C. edulis
color: yellow, pink
. <b>8--10 cm flower diameter</b>
. yellow or white petals, aging to pink
. planted on dunes and freeway embankments.  (Wikipedia has more about its <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carpobrotus_edulis#California">use in California</a>.)
