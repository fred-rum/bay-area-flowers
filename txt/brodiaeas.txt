<h2>Brodiaea spp.</h2>

Distinguishing characteristics:
<ul>
<li>Length of peduncle (flower stem).</li>
<li>Size of corolla.</li>
<li>Angle and shape of staminodes.</li>
</ul>

<a href="https://www2.palomar.edu/users/warmstrong/brosr1.htm">Labeled photos of brodiaea staminodes.</a>

<li>perianth (flower) 29&ndash;47 mm (long? wide?)</li>
<li>staminodes (crown surrounding stamens) held away from stamens, flat</li>
<li>elevation < 8000 ft.</li>
</ul>

{dwarf brodiaea-.jpg} {child:dwarf brodiaea}
<ul>
<li>peduncle (flower stem) 0.5&ndash;7 cm.</li>
<li>perianth (flower) 15&ndash;40 mm (long? wide?)</li>
<li>staminodes (crown surrounding stamens) leaning toward or parallel to stamens, tips generally reflexed, margins 1/4 incurved below tip.</li>
<li>elevation < 1500 ft.</li>
</ul>
