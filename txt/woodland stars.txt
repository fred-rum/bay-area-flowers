sci:Lithophragma spp.
x:ba

==hillside woodland star:L. heterophyllum
. hypanthium (fused calyx) base truncate
. petals with 3 lobes

==bolander's woodland star:L. heterophyllum var. bolanderi
. hypanthium base obtusely tapered or hemispheric
. petals entire or with > 3 lobes
. very rare: east bay

==san francisco woodland-star,2:L. affine
. hypanthium tapered to pedicel
. basal leaves lobed +- <= 50% to base
. most proximal cauline leaf 3-lobed
. petals white

==pink woodland star:L. parviflorum var. parviflorum
. basal leaves lobed > &frac34; to base to 3-parted
. most proximal cauline leaf 3-parted
. petals white or pink
. very rare
