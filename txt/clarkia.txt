<h3>Standard corolla shapes, buds erect or reflexed</h3>

{+winecup clarkia}
{-
<b>sepals all coming free or staying fused in pairs</b>
found everywhere
-}

{+ruby chalice clarkia}
{-
all 4 sepals staying fused
ovary and immature fruit 4-grooved
petals with +- red spot or zone at <b>base</b>
petals <b>10--30 mm</b>; stigma exserted beyond anthers
found everywhere
-}

{presidio clarkia:Clarkia franciscana}
{-
all 4 sepals staying fused
ovary and immature fruit 4-grooved
petals with +- red spot or zone at <b>base</b>
petals <b>5--13 mm</b>; stigma not exserted beyond anthers
Redwood Regional Park
-}

{farewell to spring:Clarkia amoena ssp. huntiana}
{-
all 4 sepals staying fused
ovary and immature fruit 4-grooved
petals with red spot or marks near <b>middle</b>
Sunol, scattered in the Santa Cruz range, Marin
-}

{+chaparral clarkia}
{-
all 4 sepals staying fused
ovary and immature fruit <b>8-grooved</b>
scattered around the east bay; only historically on the peninsula
-}
