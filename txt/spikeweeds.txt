==fitch's tarweed:C. fitchii

==pappose tarweed:C. parryi

==common spikeweed:C. pungens
xx:more
. leaf faces glabrous or hairy, <b>not glandular or minutely glandular</b>
.. midrin often bristly
.. margin generally bristly-ciliate, at least near the base
