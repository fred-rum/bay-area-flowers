==red fox:Vulpes vulpes
. about 7--14 pounds; longer legged than the gray fox
. <b>coat is entirely reddish</b>
. tracks have a larger gap between pads and are less distinct than coyote due to fur between toe pads
.. front and rear tracks often overlap significantly [<a href="https://www.bear-tracker.com/grayfox.html">source</a>]

==san joaquin kit fox:Vulpes macrotis ssp. mutica
. <b>about 3&frac12;--6 pounds with very large ears relative to its none#head</b>
. only observed east of the Diablo Range
