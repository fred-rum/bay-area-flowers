==trailing african daisy:D. fruticosa
color: white, pale purple, pink
. stem sometimes >> 1 m, <b>trailing to ascending</b> [example,2], sparingly branched, rooting at ground contact

==blue-and-white daisybush:D. ecklonis
bush:https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0412+0932
. stem <= 1 m, <b>erect or ascending</b>, branched distally or throughout

Jepson notes:
"Many cultivated forms are hybrids [between the two species] and combine species features; these may escape or persist from cultivation."

==cape marigold:D. sinuata
flower:https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/cgi/img_query?enlarge=0000+0000+0901+0006
. adaxial face of ray <b>yellow or orange</b>, sometimes violet at base or tip
. rare
