==trailing african daisy,2:D. fruticosa
. stem sometimes >> 1 m, <b>trailing to ascending</b>, sparingly branched, rooting at ground contact

==blue-and-white daisybush:D. ecklonis
. stem <= 1 m, <b>erect or ascending</b>, branched distally or throughout

Jepson notes:
"Many cultivated forms are hybrids [between the two species] and combine species features; these may escape or persist from cultivation."
