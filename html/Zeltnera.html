california centaury
Zeltnera venusta

  • [much branched, many flowered]

  • rare

tall centaury
Zeltnera exaltata

  • [few to many flowered]
  • pedicels generally 10–70 mm, generally > closed corollas
    • all others are 0–40 mm, generally < closed corollas
  • flower parts generally in 4s
    • all others in 5s
  • rare
