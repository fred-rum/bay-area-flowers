Clematis vitalba
buttercup family (family Ranunculaceae)

Key to Clematis spp.

  • leaflets usually 5
  • flowers in a terminal axillary panicle, i.e. in stalked clusters from the upper leaf axils
  • not in Jepson; info from here
  • rare, mostly in and near Muir Woods
