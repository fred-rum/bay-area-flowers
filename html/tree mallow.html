tree mallow

Malva arborea
mallow family (family Malvaceae)

Key to mallows (Malva spp.)

  • petals 15–20 mm with 5 dark veins
  • calyx ± 4 mm
  • bractlets widely lanceolate to roundish, bases fused to each other, free parts ± 8 mm long, > calyx
