Key to roses (Rosa spp.)

  • shrub or thicket-forming, 80 cm – 3 m

  • leaf axis ± hairy, hairs 0.2–1 mm, glandular
  • leaflets generally 5–7, ± hairy, glandular
  • sepals glandular, with toothed lateral lobes
