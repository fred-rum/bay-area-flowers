Key to blue-eyed marys (Collinsia spp.)

  • lower whorls with 1–2 flowers; upper whorls with 3+ flowers, ± crowded
  • lower pedicels ≫ calyx; upper pedicels ± = calyx
  • leaf margin generally coarsely toothed
