legume glossary

Parts of a pea flower (any flower in the legume family)

A pea flower has five petals in a bilateral arrangement (not radial).

banner
The topmost petal. It may have a crease down the middle that makes it looks like two petals stuck together, or it may be curled around the other petals. Also called the “standard petal”.
wing
Each of the two lateral petals below the banner, often fused at their top edge and enfolding the keel.
keel
The two bottom petals collectively, often fused at their bottom edge. May be partially or fully hidden by the wings.
