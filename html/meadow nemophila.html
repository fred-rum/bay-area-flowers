Nemophila pedunculata
Member of borage family (family Boraginaceae)

Key to baby blue eyes (Nemophila spp.)

  • corolla bowl- or bell-shaped, 2–8 mm wide
  • white or blue, generally dark-veined or with dark dots, may be purple-spotted at lobe tips
  • less common in the bay area
