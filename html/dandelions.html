dandelions

Taraxacum spp.
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to dandelion-like flowers

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

common dandelion
Taraxacum officinale
common dandelion
Taraxacum officinale
  • leaves ± widely lobed, toothed or sharply recurved-lobed; lobes dentate or sharply cut
  • petioles ± narrowly winged
  • outer phyllaries reflexed
  • fruit olive-brown, straw-=colored, or ± gray
  • common

red-seeded dandelion
Taraxacum erythrospermum

  • leaves sharply cut, lobes often ± toothed or with additional lobes
  • petioles slightly winged distally
  • outer phyllaries reflexed
  • fruit ± red-brown or purple
  • extremely rare

california dandelion
Taraxacum californicum

  • leaves toothed or shallowly lobed
  • leaves sessile or petioles widely winged
  • outer phyllaries erect
  • very rare
