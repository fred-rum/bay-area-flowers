california manroot

Marah fabacea
Member of gourd family (family Cucurbitaceae)
cream, white in flowering plants

Key to manroots (Marah spp.)

  • corolla rotate, yellow-green to cream or (especially inland) white
    • [When viewed from beneath, the white part of the corolla is entirely spreading. Only the green part is cupped.]
  • fruit 4–5 cm, ± spheric; prickles sparse to ± dense, < 12 mm, ± stiff, unhooked
  • Ecology: Streamsides, washes, shrubby open areas
