Collinsia bartsiifolia
Member of plantain family (family Plantaginaceae)
purple, pale purple, white in flowering plants

Key to blue-eyed marys (Collinsia spp.)

  • corolla lips ± = in fully open flowers, upper lobes ± toothed, lateral lobes notched
  • corolla throat longer than wide
  • corolla generally white to pale lavender to pink-purple
  • leaf ± thick, ± oblong, crenate, rolled under, generally finely hairy
