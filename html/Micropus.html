Micropus californicus
  • paleae ± hood-like, body thick and hard throughout, wing projected ± upward from adaxial edge, obscure, ± rolled, beak-like, ± linear
  • common

mount diablo cottonseed
Micropus amphibolus

  • paleae ± helmet-like, body thick and cartilaginous near midvein, papery otherwise, wing projected inward from near top, prominent, ± flat to concave, ± obovate
  • very rare

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.
