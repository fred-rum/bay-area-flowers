Carthamus spp.

Member of thistles
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

 woolly distaff thistle
Carthamus lanatus
woolly distaff thistle
Carthamus lanatus
  • corolla yellow
  • cauline leaves ± deeply lobed, very spiny
  • outer phyllaries generally 1.5 × inner
  • herbage loosely cobwebby to ± woolly
  • stem straw-colored
  • uncommon

smooth distaff thistle
Carthamus creticus

  • corolla yellow
  • cauline leaves ± deeply lobed, very spiny
  • outer phyllaries generally 2 × inner
  • herbage ± sparsely hairy
  • stem white
  • uncommon

safflower
Carthamus tinctorius

  • corolla yellow to red
  • cauline leaves dentate, weakly spiny
  • extremely rare in Morgan Hill
