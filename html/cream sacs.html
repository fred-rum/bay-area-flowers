cream sacs

Castilleja rubicundula var. lithospermoides
Member of broomrape family (family Orobanchaceae)

Key to cream sacs (Castilleja rubicundula)

  • according to Jepson, the corolla is only yellow
  • CalPhotos also shows yellow and cream/white
  • Mark Egger says we have a local variant with all three colors: yellow, cream, and pink
