yellow sweetclover

Melilotus officinalis
legume family (family Fabaceae)

Key to melilots and sweetclovers (Melilotus spp.)

  • corolla 4.5–7 mm, yellow
  • inflorescence axis generally 3–8 cm when flowers open
  • stem 50–200 cm
  • leaflets 1–2.5 cm, elliptic-oblong to obovate, ± toothed
  • fruit 3–5 mm, plump, irregularly cross-ridged
  • uncommon
