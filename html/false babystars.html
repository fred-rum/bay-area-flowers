false babystars

Leptosiphon androsaceus
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)

Key to baby stars, linanthus, and leptosiphons (Leptosiphon spp.)

  • corolla:
    • tube reddish, glabrous or sparsely hairy, non-glandular
    • throat violet | yellow | often white
    • lobes pink/pale purple/white
    • lobes generally > 8 mm, often abruptly soft-pointed
    • stigmas 2–6 mm and well exserted from corolla tube
  • leaf lobes 6–30 mm, tips pointed
  • calyx ciliate, nonglandular, abaxially [outer surface] generally glabrous
  • common
