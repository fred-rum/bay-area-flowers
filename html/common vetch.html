common vetch

Vicia sativa

Key features:

  • length of calyx lobes (supposedly no overlap, but measure carefully!)
  • length of corolla (supposedly no overlap, but measure carefully!)
  • length vs. width of leaflets (there is substantial overlap here)

spring vetch (Vicia sativa ssp. sativa)

  • calyx tube 6–7 mm, lobes 5–11 mm
  • corolla 18–30 mm
  • leaflets 4–10 mm wide, wedge-shaped to oblong, length 2–6 × width

narrow-leaved vetch (Vicia sativa ssp. nigra)

  • calyx tube 4.5–5.5 mm, lobes 3–4.5 mm
  • corolla 10–18 mm
  • leaflets 5–7 mm wide, linear to generally lance-oblong, length 4–10 × width
