california aster

Corethrogyne filaginifolia
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to white daisies

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

Jepson lists only one Corethrogyne species in California. Various taxonomists have partitioned the California populations into multiple species and variants, but Jepson explicitly rejects that notion. CalFlora lists two species in California, but POWO treats the other species as a synonym of this one. In any case, CalFlora lists only one species in the bay area.
