Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)
pale purple in flowering plants

Key to rabbit-tobaccos (Pseudognaphalium spp.)

  • Stem stalked-glandular, ± tomentose
  • Leaves crowded proximally or not
  • Leaf base not clasping, decurrent 2–10 mm
  • Leaf faces ± green or thinly gray-tomentose, stalked-glandular
  • Leaf margin curled under and closely wavy
  • Inflorescence in panicle-like cluster
  • phyllaries generally ± pink [it’s distinctive in CalPhotos]
  • common
