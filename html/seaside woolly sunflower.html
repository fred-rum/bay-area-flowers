seaside woolly sunflower

Eriophyllum staechadifolium
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to woolly sunflowers (Eriophyllum spp.)

  • subshrub, much branched, 30–150 cm
  • peduncle < 1 cm
  • 8–11 phyllaries, barely overlapping
  • 6–9 rays, 3–5 mm
  • Ecology: coastal dunes, bluffs, coastal scrub
