false solomon’s seals

Maianthemum spp.
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

Key features:

  • 4 or 6 perianth parts
  • perianth > stamens or ≪ stamens
  • leaves with or without petioles

false solomon’s seal
Maianthemum racemosum
  • Perianth parts 6
  • Inflorescence is a panicle
  • > 20 flowers per inflorescence
  • perianth 1–2 mm, ≪ stamens
  • fruit tan with red speckles
  • > 5 leaves, 7–20 cm, ovate to oblong-elliptic; petioles ± 0
  • common
star-flowered lily-of-the-valley
Maianthemum stellatum
star-flowered lily-of-the-valley
Maianthemum stellatum
  • Perianth parts 6
  • Inflorescence is a raceme (or few-branched near base)
  • 5–15 flowers per inflorescence
  • perianth 4–7 mm, > stamens
  • fruit tan with red speckles and bold dark purple stripes
  • > 5 leaves, 5–17 cm, (ob)lanceolate to elliptic; petioles 0
  • common

western lily of the valley
Maianthemum dilatatum

  • Perianth parts 4
  • Inflorescence is a raceme
  • 10–50 flowers per inflorescence
  • 2–3 leaves, 5–20 cm, cordate to sagittate; petioles 5–15 cm
  • San Bruno, Marin, Mt. Tam
