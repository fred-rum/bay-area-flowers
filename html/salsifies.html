salsifies

Tragopogon spp.
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to tribe Cichorieae

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

purple salsify
Tragopogon porrifolius
purple salsify
Tragopogon porrifolius
  • rays purple
  • 5–11 phyllaries, ≥ flowers when fully bloomed
  • pappus ± brown
yellow salsify
Tragopogon dubius
yellow salsify
Tragopogon dubius
  • rays pale lemon-yellow
  • 8–13 phyllaries, much exceeding flowers when fully bloomed [but not exceeding the seed ball]
  • pappus ± white
