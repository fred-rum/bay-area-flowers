sacramento valley buttercup

Ranunculus canus var. canus
buttercup family (family Ranunculaceae)

Key to buttercups (Ranunculus spp.)

There are no other members of this species in the bay area.

  • stem erect to decumbent
  • 5–7 petals, 6–12 mm long, 3–6 mm wide, yellow
  • receptacle glabrous
  • basal and proximal cauline leaves 3-parted, segments 1–3-lobed
  • Mission Peak, Sunol, Mt. Diablo

Chris’s observations: none
