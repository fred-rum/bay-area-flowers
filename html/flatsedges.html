flatsedges

fragrant flatsedge
Cyperus odoratus

  • fruit body 1.5–3 × longer than wide
  • flower bract 2–4 mm
  • fruit 1.5–2.4 mm
  • stem base not thickened
  • spikelet axis jointed at each bract
  • very rare

straw-colored flatsedge
Cyperus strigosus

  • fruit body 1.5–3 × longer than wide
  • flower bract 2–4 mm
  • fruit 1.5–2.4 mm
  • stem base thickened
  • spikelet axis continuous
  • extremely rare at Wilder Ranch near ocean
