Member of borage family (family Boraginaceae)
pale blue, pale purple in flowering plants

Key to baby blue eyes (Nemophila menziesii)

  • corolla blue or pale purple with a nearly white center
    • outer colored section often has darker colored veins
    • inner white section often has even darker dots tracing the path of veins
