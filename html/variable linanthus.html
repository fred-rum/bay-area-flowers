variable linanthus

Leptosiphon parviflorus
Member of phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)
purple, pale purple, pink, yellow, white in flowering plants

Key to baby stars, linanthus, and leptosiphons (Leptosiphon spp.)

  • corolla:
    • tube maroon/pink/yellow, hairy, glandular
    • throat yellow/orange/purple
    • lobes pink/yellow/purple/white, sometimes with red/purple spots at the base [Jepson says “often”, CalPhotos says “almost never”]
    • lobes 4–8 mm
    • stigmas 1–7 mm and strongly exserted from corolla tube
  • leaf lobes 2–18 mm, middle lobe tip acute
  • calyx densely hairy, glandular
  • common
