tufted poppy

Eschscholzia caespitosa
poppy family (family Papaveraceae)

Key to Eschscholzia spp.

  • has no more than an indistinct disc below the corolla (receptacle rim ≤ 0.3 mm)
  • its recepticle slants smoothly down to the width of the stem
  • can be found at Pinnacles NP, but is extremely rare in the bay area
