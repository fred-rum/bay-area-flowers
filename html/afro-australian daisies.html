afro-australian daisies

Dimorphotheca

This is most likely D. fruticosa or D. ecklonis. The flower heads evidently are identical. Jepson distinguishes them mostly by stem:
  • D. ecklonis: stem <= 1 m, erect or ascending, branched distally or throughout
  • D. fruticosa: stem sometimes >> 1 m, trailing to ascending, sparingly branched, rooting at ground contact
Jepson further notes: "Many cultivated forms are hybrids [between the two species] and combine species features; these may escape or persist from cultivation."
