Heliotropium curassavicum
  • stem prostrate to ± ascending, glabrous
  • calyx lobes glabrous
  • corolla white, throat generally purple or yellow
  • scattered everywhere

Heliotropium europaeum

  • stem ascending to erect, puberulent to short-soft-hairy
  • calyx lobes bristly
  • corolla white
  • rare along the Pacific coast
