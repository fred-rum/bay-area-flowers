silk tassel

Garrya spp.
Member of family Garryaceae

Except for old historical records and extremely rare plants that I don't expect to encounter, there are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

Key features:

  • abaxial leaf hairs
  • fruit hairs are also useful, but perhaps cannot fully distinguish species on their own
