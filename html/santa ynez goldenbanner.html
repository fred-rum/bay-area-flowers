santa ynez goldenbanner

Thermopsis macrophylla
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)

Key to goldenbanners (Thermopsis spp.)

  • plant 80–250 cm
  • stem stout, erect
  • very rare, and only in Santa Barbara County
    • “Our study of type material indicates that this name has been erroneously applied to the more common T. californica var. californica”; Chen, 1994. [pdf]
