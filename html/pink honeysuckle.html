pink honeysuckle

Lonicera hispidula
Member of honeysuckle family (family Caprifoliaceae)

Key to honeysuckles (Lonicera spp.)

  • flowers in ± interrupted spikes on axillary peduncles or at ends of branches
  • upper leaf pairs fused around stem
  • corolla glandular-hairy or hairy [may be short and sparse]
  • stipules ± conspicuous, at least near inflorescence
  • pink flowers
  • common in bay area
