lesser chickweed

Stellaria pallida
pink family (family Caryophyllaceae)

Key to chickweeds (Stellaria spp.)

  • leaves ± evenly spaced
  • internode hairs in a line
  • no petals
  • sepals 2–3 mm
  • very rare
