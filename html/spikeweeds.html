spikeweeds

Centromadia spp.
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to tarweeds and allies (tribe Madieae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

fitch’s tarweed
Centromadia fitchii
fitch’s tarweed
Centromadia fitchii
  • leaf densely coated with short hairs, stalked-glandular with yellow, brown, or black glands

pappose tarweed
Centromadia parryi

  • leaf glabrous or hairy, occasionally with sessile yellow glands

common spikeweed
Centromadia pungens

  • leaf faces glabrous or hairy, not glandular or minutely glandular
    • midrin often bristly
    • margin generally bristly-ciliate, at least near the base
