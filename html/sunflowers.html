sunflowers

sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

For the purposes of this page, "sunflowers" are defined as a plant in the sunflower family (family Asteraceae) in which the large head is composed of disk flowers surrounded by a ring of ray flowers. This page collects the sunflowers in one place for easier comparison.

See also yellow daisies and white daisies for similar plants with smaller heads.
