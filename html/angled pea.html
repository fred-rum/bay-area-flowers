angled pea

Lathyrus angulatus
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)

Key to sweet peas and vetchlings (Lathyrus spp.)

  • 1 flower in inflorescence
  • 2 leaflets per leaf
  • corolla 9–12 mm, lavender or purple
  • inflorescence axis extended as bristle beyond flower
  • calyx tube ± ≤ lobes
