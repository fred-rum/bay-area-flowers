cut-leaved crane's-bill
Geranium dissectum
  • stem hairs hairs rough, spreading to reflexed
  • leaf blade 2.5–5.4 cm wide, divided 75–95% to base, segments 5–7, rhombic
  • petals 2.9–5.8 mm, ± notched, rose-purple

dove's-foot crane's-bill
Geranium molle
dove's-foot crane's-bill
Geranium molle
  • stem hairs ± sparse, soft
  • leaf blade 0.9–5.2 cm wide, divided 50–75% to base, segments 7–9, obtriangular
  • petals 3–10.5 mm, notched, red-purple

round-leaved crane's-bill
Geranium rotundifolium
round-leaved crane's-bill
Geranium rotundifolium
  • stem hairs rough, spreading
  • leaf blade 1.9–4.4 cm wide; divided 55–65% to base, segments 5–7, obtriangular
  • petals 6–7 mm, rounded, rose-purple

herb robert
Geranium robertianum
herb robert
Geranium robertianum
  • stem hairs ± sparse
  • leaf blade 3.5–10 cm wide; divided > 0.9 to base or midrib, segments 5, ± sessile to short-stalked, pinnately lobed to dissected
  • petals 10–14 mm, rounded, pink to red-purple [often shorter]
  • anthers ± purple [yellow for G. purpureum]
