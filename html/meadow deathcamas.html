Toxicoscordion venenosum
trillium family (family Melanthiaceae)

Key to deathcamas (Toxicoscordion spp.)

This species has subspecies or variants that don't seem worth distinguishing.

  • petals 4–6 mm
  • stamens usually = perianth, occasionally >
  • pedicels in fruit 3–25 mm, generally ascending (10–60° from axis)
  • plant glabrous
  • rare
