currants and gooseberries

Ribes spp.
gooseberry family (family Grossulariaceae)

Caution: There may be other species of this genus not yet included in this guide.

Key features:

  • spines on twig nodes or not
  • sepal color, spread angle, and number
  • styles hairy at base or glabrous
  • stamen length
  • leaf glandular on underside or not
  • bristles between nodes on twigs or not

I’ve made very few currant/gooseberry observations, and my identifications are not firm.
