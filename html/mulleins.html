mulleins

figwort family (family Scrophulariaceae)

Except for historical records that I'm ignoring, there are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

  • uncommon

  • all filaments densely purple-hairy
  • rare

wand mullein

  • leaf ± hairy
  • all filaments densely purple-hairy
  • very rare
