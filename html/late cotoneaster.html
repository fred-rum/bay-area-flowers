Cotoneaster coriaceus
Member of rose family (family Rosaceae)

Key to cotoneasters (Cotoneaster spp.)

  • plant arching, 1–9 m
  • petals white, spreading; fruit bright red
  • leaf blade 35–95 mm, 17–45 mm wide, thick, adaxially dark green, lateral veins often sunken
  • common
