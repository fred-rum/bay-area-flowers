indian paintbrushes

broomrape family (family Orobanchaceae)

Key to paintbrushes (Castilleja spp.)

Key features:

  • hairs woolly and much-branched vs. glabrous/puberulent/bristly and unbranched vs. glandular proximal to inflorescence
  • leaf shape (linear, lanceolate, or oblong/rounded), wavy-margined or not
  • corolla drooping out through calyx sinus or not

Perennial herb to subshrub
stems generally branched
generally drier places

woolly indian paintbrush
Castilleja foliolosa
woolly indian paintbrush
Castilleja foliolosa
  • hairs woolly, felt-like, much-branched, ± white to ± gray
  • leaves 10–50 mm, ± linear; lobes 0–3, tips obtuse; generally puberulent or glabrous (bristly)
coast indian paintbrush
Castilleja affinis
coast indian paintbrush
Castilleja affinis
  • ± glabrous to bristly-puberulent, generally nonglandular
  • herbage not both with branched hairs and gray-green to white-woolly
  • calyx long-nonglandular- and short-glandular-hairy
  • corolla not curved out through abaxial side of calyx sinus
  • leaves 20–80 mm, ± lanceolate, generally not fleshy; lobes 0–5, tips ± rounded; generally puberulent or glabrous (bristly)
monterey indian paintbrush
Castilleja latifolia
monterey indian paintbrush
Castilleja latifolia
  • herbage not both with branched hairs and gray-green to white-woolly
  • corolla not curved out through abaxial side of calyx sinus
  • leaves ± fleshy, oblong to rounded, lobes 0–3, truncate-rounded; hairs generally bristly or shaggy
  • common on the peninsula + Alum Rock
wavyleaf paintbrush
Castilleja applegatei
wavyleaf paintbrush
Castilleja applegatei
  • herbage not both with branched hairs and gray-green to white-woolly
  • corolla not curved out through abaxial side of calyx sinus
  • leaves 20–70 mm, ± lanceolate, generally wavy-margined
  • plant glandular-puberulent to -sticky-hairy proximal to inflorescence
  • Marin, Mt. Diablo, Sierra Azul, and scattered outside the bay area
franciscan paintbrush
Castilleja subinclusa ssp. franciscana
franciscan paintbrush
Castilleja subinclusa ssp. franciscana
  • herbage not both with branched hairs and gray-green to white-woolly
  • corolla (including lower lip) generally curved out through abaxial calyx sinus [e.g. the yellow-green corolla droops out of the red calyx]
  • leaves 30–80 mm, lanceolate; lobes 0–3
  • peninsula from Thornewood north and east bay from Chabot north
wight's paint brush
Castilleja wightii
wight's paint brush
Castilleja wightii
  • herbage not both with branched hairs and gray-green to white-woolly
  • corolla not curved out through abaxial side of calyx sinus
  • plant glandular-puberulent to -sticky-hairy close to inflorescence

small-flower indian-paintbrush
Castilleja minor

  • annual
  • stem ± simple
  • wet places
  • leaves and bracts entire, lance-linear
  • Sierra Azul, Mt. Tam
