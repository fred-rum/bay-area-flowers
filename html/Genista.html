Genista spp.

legume family (family Fabaceae)

Key to brooms

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

Stem generally ribbed or angled, green.

ternately 1-compound leaves on older stems
yellow corolla

french broom
Genista monspessulana
french broom
Genista monspessulana
  • leaflet not narrow (length < 5 × width), margins flat
  • leaflet generally 10–15 mm
  • stipules < 2 mm, deciduous
  • racemes axillary
  • very common

madeira broom
Genista maderensis

  • leaflet 10–15 mm
  • stipules 3–6 mm, persistent
  • racemes terminal
  • extremely rare

canary island broom
Genista canariensis

  • leaflet 5-10(12) mm
  • extremely rare

bridal veil broom
Genista monosperma

  • simple leaves
  • white corolla
  • extremely rare
