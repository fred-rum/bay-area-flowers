Single-color gilias

Gilia achilleifolia
  • flower head hemispheric or 1–8 flowers
  • corolla 5–21 mm, purple to white

Gilia capitata
  • flower head spheric with 25–100 flowers

Multi-color gilias

bird’s-eye gilia
Gilia tricolor
bird’s-eye gilia
Gilia tricolor
  • many branches from base, spreading
  • open clusters with 2–5 flowers
  • corolla 10–19 mm, blue-violet at tip, paler below
  • corolla throat yellow or orange with large purple spots, possibly merging into a ring
photo
 purplespot gilia
Gilia clivorum
purplespot gilia
Gilia clivorum
  • generally many branches, spreading or erect
  • open clusters with 2–5 flowers
  • corolla 6–8 mm, purple to white (may fade to white near throat)
  • corolla throat yellow with large purple spots
