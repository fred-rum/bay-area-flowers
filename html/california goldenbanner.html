california goldenbanner
Thermopsis californica var. californica

  • stipules ovate to widely ovate
  • inflorescence bracts < 15 mm wide
  • leaflet hairs soft, not appressed
  • uncommon

silvery false-lupine
Thermopsis californica var. argentata

  • stipules lanceolate to ovate
  • inflorescence bracts < 6 mm wide
  • leaflet silver; hairs long, soft, straight, appressed
  • extremely rare
