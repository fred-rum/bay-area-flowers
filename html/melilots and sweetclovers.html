melilots and sweetclovers

Melilotus spp.
legume family (family Fabaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

small melilot
Melilotus indicus
small melilot
Melilotus indicus
  • corolla 2.5–3 mm, yellow
  • inflorescence axis generally 1–2 cm when flowers open
  • stem 10–60 cm
  • leaflets 1–2.5 cm, oblanceolate to wedge-shaped-obovate, ± toothed
  • fruit 2–3 mm, bumpy or with faint lines
  • common
yellow sweetclover
Melilotus officinalis
  • corolla 4.5–7 mm, yellow
  • inflorescence axis generally 3–8 cm when flowers open
  • stem 50–200 cm
  • leaflets 1–2.5 cm, elliptic-oblong to obovate, ± toothed
  • fruit 3–5 mm, plump, irregularly cross-ridged
  • uncommon

white sweetclover
Melilotus albus

  • corolla 3.5–5 mm, white
  • inflorescence axis generally 3–8 cm when flowers open
  • stem 50–200 cm
  • leaflets 1–2.5 cm, elliptic-oblong to obovate, ± toothed
  • fruit 3–5 mm, with network of lines
  • uncommon
