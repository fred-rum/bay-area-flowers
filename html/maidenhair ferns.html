Key to ferns (class Polypodiopsida)

california maidenhair fern
Adiantum jordanii
california maidenhair fern
Adiantum jordanii
  • frond is 2–3-pinnate [terminal leaflets are palmate]
  • terminal leaflets are cut/lobed often < ¼ way to base, margins converging at 90–180° angle

black maidenhair fern
Adiantum capillus-veneris

  • frond is 2–3-pinnate [final division is palmate]
  • terminal leaflets are cut/lobed often > ¼ to ⅔ way to base, margins converging at 45–90° angle
five-finger fern
Adiantum aleuticum
five-finger fern
Adiantum aleuticum
  • frond is palmate-pinnate [can have more than 5 “fingers”]
