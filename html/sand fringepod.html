sand fringepod

Thysanocarpus curvipes
mustard family (family Brassicaceae)

Key to fringepods (Thysanocarpus spp.)

  • proximal pedicels in fruit recurved in a continuous arc, 3--7(12) mm
  • fruit 3--6(9) mm wide
  • cauline leaves lanceolate, bases lobed, clasping
  • basal leaves present
  • fertile anthers purple
  • common
