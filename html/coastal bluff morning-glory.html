coastal bluff morning-glory

Calystegia purpurata ssp. saxicola
bindweed family (family Convolvulaceae)

Key to pacific false bindweed (Calystegia purpurata)

  • stem trailing to weakly climbing, generally < 1 m
  • leaf ovate-triangular to reniform, tip generally rounded to notched, sinus generally ± closed
  • bracts generally with small lobes
  • Ecology: Rocky coastal scrub
  • very rare

Chris’s observations: none
