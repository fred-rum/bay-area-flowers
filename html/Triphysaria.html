Triphysaria spp.

Member of broomrape family (family Orobanchaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

 johnnytuck
Triphysaria eriantha
johnnytuck
Triphysaria eriantha
  • plant ± purple
  • corolla 10–25 mm, > bract, yellow or white fading to pale purple
  • beak dark purple
  • pouches 3–4 mm deep

purplebeak owl’s-clover
Triphysaria micrantha

  • plant ± purple
  • corolla 8–15 mm, ± = bract, yellow
  • beak dark purple
  • pouches 1–2 mm deep

yellow-beak false owl’s-clover
Triphysaria versicolor

  • plant green-brown to yellow-brown [i.e. greenish]
  • corolla 12–22 mm, yellow or white fading to pale purple
  • beak ± yellow
  • pouches 2–4 mm deep

san francisco owl’s-clover
Triphysaria floribunda

  • plant yellow-brown [green]
  • corolla 10–14 mm, cream, stamens exserted
    • all other stamens are included
  • north peninsula

dwarf orthocarpus
Triphysaria pusilla

  • plant yellow-brown or ± purple
  • corolla 4–7 mm, ± purple
    • all other corollas are ≥ 8 mm
