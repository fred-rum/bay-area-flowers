oak mistletoe

Phoradendron villosum
sandalwood family (family Santalaceae)

Key to leafy mistletoes (Phoradendron spp.)

  • leaf length generally < 1.5 × width, 15–60 mm, generally > 20 mm
  • stem generally short-hairy, at least at tip
  • spike with 2–5(7) fertile internodes, pistillate 6–15(20)-flowered
  • on woody plants except conifers
  • leaf ≤ 3 cm, width ≤ 2 cm
  • leaf gray-green, dull
  • common
