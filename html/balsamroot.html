california balsamroot
Balsamorhiza macrolepis
  • basal leaf pinnately divided into linear to ovate segments
  • a few cauline leaves are present, but not described
  • scattered in the Diablo range, but not in the Mt. Diablo area

deltoid balsamroot
Balsamorhiza deltoidea

  • basal entire to coarsely dentate
  • cauline leaves entire
  • rare in the Mt. Diablo area
