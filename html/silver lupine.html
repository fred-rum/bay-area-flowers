silver lupine

Lupinus albifrons
legume family (family Fabaceae)

Key to lupines (Lupinus spp.)

There are no other members of this species in the bay area.

Lupinus albifrons var. albifrons
silver bush lupine
Lupinus albifrons var. albifrons
  • shrub 50 cm–5 m; woody ± throughout; trunk is generally distinct
  • bract 4–8 mm

douglas’ silver lupine
Lupinus albifrons var. douglasii

  • shrub 1–2 m; woody ± throughout
  • bract 10–24 mm

silver lupine
Lupinus albifrons var. collinus

  • subshrub, 20–40 cm; only base and some lower branches are woody
  • bract 7–9 mm
