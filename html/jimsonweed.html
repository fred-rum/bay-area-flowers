Datura stramonium

Key to devil’s trumpets (Datura spp.)

  • corolla white or pale purple, 6–9 cm, lobes 0.8–1.0 cm
    • style 4–6 cm
  • calyx 3.5–4.5 cm, angled toward base, lobes 0.5–0.7 cm
  • fruit with 0.3–1.0 cm prickles and black seeds
