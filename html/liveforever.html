liveforever

Dudleya spp.
stonecrop family (family Crassulaceae)

Except for old historical records and extremely rare plants that I don't expect to encounter, there are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

bluff lettuce
Dudleya farinosa

  • petals pale yellow [not full yellow]
  • stem often > 8 cm long, 10–30 mm wide
  • peduncle 10–35 cm
  • leaf 2.5–6 cm

canyon live-forever
Dudleya cymosa
canyon live-forever
Dudleya cymosa
  • petals bright yellow, orange, or red
  • plant generally < 10 cm wide
  • leaves 0.8–3 × longer than wide

coast dudleya
Dudleya caespitosa

  • petals bright yellow
  • plant 8–32 cm wide
  • leaves 3–10 × longer than wide
  • stem of older plants often exposed between dried leaves

lanceleaf liveforever
Dudleya lanceolata

  • petals yellow to generally red
  • plant 3–35 cm wide
  • leaves 3–10 × longer than wide
  • stem of older plants covered by dried leaves
