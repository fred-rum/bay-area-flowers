selfing willowherb

Epilobium cleistogamum
Member of evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)

Key to willowherbs (Epilobium spp.)

  • 15–32 cm tall
  • leaf 15–55 mm, linear to narrowly elliptic; distal leaves hairy, often folded along midrib
  • infloescence leafy, spreading and glandular hairy
  • hypanthium 0.5–1 mm
  • petals 2–5.5 mm, white to pale pink
  • very rare: Henry Coe
