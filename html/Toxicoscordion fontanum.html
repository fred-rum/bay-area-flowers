Toxicoscordion fontanum
trillium family (family Melanthiaceae)

Key to deathcamas (Toxicoscordion spp.)

  • petals 4–15 mm
  • stamens ± = perianth
  • leaf erect, often > stem
  • inflorescence narrow, often pyramid-shaped in youth (example)
  • rare
