Sisymbrium spp.

mustard family (family Brassicaceae)

Key to mustards

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

hedge mustard

hedge mustard
Sisymbrium officinale
  • petals 2.5–4 mm long, 1–2 mm wide
  • sepals 2–2.5 mm
  • reflexed-hairy
  • uncommon

london rocket
Sisymbrium irio

  • petals 2.5–3.5 mm (claw 1–1.5 mm), 1–1.5 mm wide
  • sepals 2–2.5 mm, not horned
  • glabrous or sparsely hairy [only] at base
  • rare

tumble mustard
Sisymbrium altissimum

  • petals 6–8 mm (claw 3.5–6 mm), 2.5–4 mm wide
  • very rare

eastern rocket
Sisymbrium orientale

  • petals 7–9 mm long (claw 3–5.5 mm), 2.5–4 mm wide
  • rare, mostly around SF
