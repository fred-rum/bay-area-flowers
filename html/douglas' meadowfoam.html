douglas’ meadowfoam

Limnanthes douglasii
Member of meadowfoam family (family Limnanthaceae)

Except for extremely rare plants that I don't expect to encounter, there are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

There are no other members of this species anywhere.

Limnanthes douglasii ssp. rosea

  • petals white, veined purple or green
  • anthers cream, dark pink, orange-red, or ± black

Limnanthes douglasii ssp. douglasii

  • petals yellow with white tips
  • anthers yellow, cream, or red-brown

  • found only at Moss Beach (Half Moon Bay)
