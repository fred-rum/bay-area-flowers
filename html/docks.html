docks

Rumex spp.

6 perianth lobes, but in fruit outer 3 ± inconspicuous, inner 3 enlarged, hardened, ± veiny, midrib of 1–3 often expanded into tubercle [round lump on flower]

Key features:

  • arrangement of stems (from base or middle and/or stems among inflorescences)
  • shape and length of leaf
  • length/width of inner perianth lobe, dentate or not

sheep's sorrel
Rumex acetosella
sheep's sorrel
Rumex acetosella
  • leaf blade hastate (spearhead-shaped) or sagittate (arrowhead-shaped) or base tapered
    • (other Rumex species are not hastate or sagittate (but may have a tapered base)
  • several stems from base
  • no perianth tubercles
  • very common

  • stem generally ± erect
  • generally unbranched below inflorescence (inflorescence may be branched, however) (example illustration)

Inner perianth lobe margin generally entire (if toothed or notched, teeth or notches < 0.3 mm)

curly dock
Rumex crispus
curly dock
Rumex crispus
  • inner perianth lobe margin generally entire (if toothed or notched, teeth or notches < 0.3 mm)
  • inflorescence generally dense
  • pedicels (3)4–8 mm
  • inner perianth lobes 3.5–6 mm, 3–5 mm wide
  • tubercles generally 3, at least 1 larger, > 1 mm wide, but all generally ≪ 1/3 inner perianth lobe width [illustration]
  • leaf margin strongly wavy, especially near base
  • very common

clustered dock
clustered dock
  • inner perianth lobe margin generally entire (if toothed or notched, teeth or notches < 0.3 mm)
  • inflorescence ± open (between whorls)
  • pedicels 1–4(5) mm, generally ± ≥ inner perianth
  • inner perianth lobes 2–3 mm, 1–1.6(2) mm wide
  • tubercles 3, ± equal, almost as wide as lobe [illustration]
  • leaf margin ± flat
  • common

Inner perianth lobe margin variously dentate, at least some teeth ≥ 0.3 mm [illustration]

fiddle dock
Rumex pulcher
fiddle dock
Rumex pulcher
  • perennial
  • stem: erect to arching at tip, 2–6(7) dm, branched, slender
  • leaf: blade 4–10(15) cm, (2)3–5 cm wide
  • inflorescence: branches spreading
  • pedicels: 2–5(6) mm, thick
  • inner perianth lobes 3–6 mm, 2–3 mm wide except teeth, margin generally dentate (± entire) with 2–5(9) teeth, 0.3–2.5 mm, generally on both sides [illustration]
  • 3(1) tubercles, generally warty
  • common

broad-leaved dock
Rumex obtusifolius
  • perennial
  • stem: 6–12(15) dm, generally branched
  • leaf: blade 20–40 cm, 10–15 cm wide, base generally cordate, margin generally entire, flat
  • inflorescence: branches generally ascending
  • pedicels: 2.5–8.5(10) mm, thin
  • inner perianth lobe 3–6 mm, 2–3.5 mm wide except teeth, margin generally dentate with 2–5 teeth, 0.5–1.8 mm
  • 3 tubercles, 1 larger (2 may be inconspicuous/absent), smooth
  • scattered
