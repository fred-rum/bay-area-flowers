flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

stinging nettle
Urtica dioica
  • 50–300 cm tall
  • leaf blade 6–20 cm
  • inflorescence raceme- or panicle-like, generally > petiole

dwarf nettle
Urtica urens

  • 10–60(90) cm tall
  • leaf blade 1.8–4(6) cm
  • inflorescence generally ± head- or spike-like, often < petiole
