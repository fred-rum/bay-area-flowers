bolander’s woodland star

Lithophragma heterophyllum var. bolanderi
saxifrage family (family Saxifragaceae)

Key to woodland stars (Lithophragma spp.)

  • hypanthium base obtusely tapered or hemispheric
  • petals entire or with > 3 lobes
  • very rare: east bay

Chris’s observations: none
