shield ferns

Polystichum spp.
shieldfern family (family Dryopteridaceae)

Key to ferns

Except for historical records that I'm ignoring, there are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

western sword fern
Polystichum munitum
western sword fern
Polystichum munitum
  • frond 1-pinnate
  • stipe base scales ovate, ± 3(6) mm wide
    • those above proximal pinnae generally > 1 mm wide, persistent
  • very common

narrowleaf sword fern
Polystichum imbricans

  • frond 1-pinnate
  • stipe base scales lanceolate, ± 2–3 mm wide
    • those above proximal pinnae generally < 1 mm wide, falling early
  • rare

california shield fern
Polystichum californicum

  • frond generally 2-pinnate, but pinnules range from 0% to 100% divided
  • pinnules gradually tapered to attachment < 3 mm wide [or not fully divided]
  • common

dudley's shield fern
Polystichum dudleyi

  • frond 2-pinnate
  • pinnules abruptly narrowed to attachment < 0.5 mm wide
  • common
