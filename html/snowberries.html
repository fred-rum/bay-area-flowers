snowberries

Symphoricarpos spp.
honeysuckle family (family Caprifoliaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

Key features:

  • plant height
  • number of berries in each cluster
common snowberry
Symphoricarpos albus
common snowberry
Symphoricarpos albus
  • plant 60–180 cm, erect
  • corolla swollen on 1 side, with 5 nectary glands within swelling
  • inflorescence generally 8–16-flowered
creeping snowberry
Symphoricarpos mollis
creeping snowberry
Symphoricarpos mollis
  • Plant 15–60 cm, sprawling
  • corolla scarcely or not swollen, 5 nectary glands, 1 below each corolla lobe
  • inflorescence generally 2–8-flowered
