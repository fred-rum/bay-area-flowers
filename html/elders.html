elders

Sambucus spp.
Member of viburnum family (family Viburnaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

There is a general difference between the leaves (blue elder is bowed), but it is not consistent enough to be used for identification.

blue elder
Sambucus cerulea
blue elder
Sambucus cerulea
  • inflorescence ± flat-topped, central axis generally not dominant
  • petals ± spreading
  • glaucus blue berries

Formerly a subspecies of black elderberry, but now elevated to its own species.

red-berried elder
Sambucus racemosa
red-berried elder
Sambucus racemosa
  • inflorescence ± dome-shaped, central axis generally dominant
  • petals often reflexed
  • red berries
