large-flowered leptosiphon

Leptosiphon grandiflorus
phlox family (family Polemoniaceae)

Key to baby stars, linanthus, and leptosiphons (Leptosiphon spp.)

  • corolla ± sessile above densely bracted head (corolla starts spreading within bracts or not much above)
  • corolla 15–30 mm:
    • throat yellow
    • lobes white blending to pink/pale purple/white
  • uncommon
