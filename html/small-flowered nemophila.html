small-flowered nemophila

Nemophila parviflora
borage family (family Boraginaceae)

Key to baby blue eyes (Nemophila spp.)

  • corolla bell-shaped, 1–5 mm wide
  • white or blue, without dark veins or dots
  • style < 2 mm
  • lower leaves cut or shallow-lobed with dissimilar lobes, merging or not stalked
  • scattered throughout the bay area
