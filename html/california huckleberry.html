california huckleberry

Vaccinium ovatum
heather family (family Ericaceae)

Key to blueberries (Vaccinium spp.)

Caution: There are members of this species not yet included in this guide.

  • leaves evergreen, leathery, veins not prominent abaxially
  • inflorescence (and fruit) dense, axillary
  • pedicel jointed to flower
  • filaments ± hairy
  • twigs not angled, ± gray [often reddish distally]
  • fruit black [or red or ?blue?], glaucous or not
