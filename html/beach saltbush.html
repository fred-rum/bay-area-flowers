Key to saltbushes (Atriplex spp.)

  • perennial herb/subshrub, prostrate to decumbent, < 30 cm [tall?], up to 100 cm long
  • leaf sessile, entire, elliptic to wide-ovate
  • one of the more common of the saltbushes
