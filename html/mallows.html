mallows

Malva spp.
mallow family (family Malvaceae)

Key to mallows and checkermallows

bull mallow
Malva nicaeensis
bull mallow
Malva nicaeensis
  • petals 5–12 mm; sepals 4–6 mm; petals ± > or ≫ sepals
  • petal veins generally dark
  • bractlets wider than linear, bases fused to each other or to sepals

dwarf mallow
Malva neglecta

  • petals generally twice as long as sepals; sepals 4–6 mm
  • petal veins not dark
  • bractlets widely linear to narrowly lanceolate, bases free from each other and from sepals

cheeseweed mallow
Malva parviflora

  • petals 3–5 mm; sepals ~3 mm
  • bractlets 1–2 mm long, linear to thread-like

common mallow
Malva sylvestris

  • petals generally 15–30 mm; sepals 6–8 mm
  • petal veins dark
  • bractlets 3–7 mm long, 2–4 mm wide, free
