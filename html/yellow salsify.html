yellow salsify

Tragopogon dubius
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to salsifies (Tragopogon spp.)

  • rays pale lemon-yellow
  • 8–13 phyllaries, much exceeding flowers when fully bloomed [but not exceeding the seed ball]
  • pappus ± white
