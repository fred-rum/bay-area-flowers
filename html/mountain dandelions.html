annual false dandelion
Agoseris heterophylla var. heterophyla

  • stem often leafy above base
  • ligules 2–6 mm, ± equaling involucre [the phyllaries reach almost to the petal tips]
  • leaf petiole not purple
  • common

bigflower agoseris
Agoseris grandiflora var. grandiflora

  • stem generally not leafy or obscurely leafy above base
  • ligule 3–7 mm, ± equaling involucre
  • petiole generally purple
  • very common

coast range agoseris
Agoseris hirsuta
  • stem generally not leafy or obscurely leafy above base
  • ligule 6–16 mm, much exceeding involucre
  • leaf petiole rarely purple
  • uncommon

spearleaf agoseris
Agoseris retrorsa

  • stem generally not leafy or obscurely leafy above base
  • ligule 6–15 mm, exceeding (equaling) involucre
  • petiole ± purple
  • rare

seaside agoseris
Agoseris apargioides

  • stem generally not leafy or obscurely leafy above base
  • ligules 8–16 mm, much exceeding involucre
  • petiole ± purple
  • coastal dunes and sand hills [all others avoid sand]
  • uncommon
