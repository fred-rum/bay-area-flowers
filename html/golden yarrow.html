golden yarrow

Eriophyllum confertiflorum
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to woolly sunflowers (Eriophyllum spp.)

  • subshrub or shrub, generally 20–70 cm
  • 4–7 phyllaries, strongly overlapping
  • peduncle 0–2.5(9) cm
  • 4–6 (0–8) rays, 2–5 mm
  • Ecology: Many dry habitats
