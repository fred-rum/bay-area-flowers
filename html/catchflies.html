small-flowered catchfly
Silene gallica
small-flowered catchfly
Silene gallica
  • corolla white to pale purple
  • calyx tube clearly with 10 veins
  • pedicel and calyx hairy
  • common

sleepy catchfly
Silene antirrhina

  • corolla white in the center, pale purple at the adaxial tips, dark red purple abaxially
  • calyx tube clearly with 10 veins
  • pedicel and calyx glabrous
  • rare

multinerved catchfly
Silene coniflora

  • corolla white to pale purple
  • calyx tube clearly with 16–30 veins
  • very rare

perennial herb with woody caudex

north californian indian pink
Silene laciniata ssp. californica
north californian indian pink
Silene laciniata ssp. californica
  • corolla bright red
  • calyx tube with 10 faint veins
  • common

scouler’s catchfly
Silene scouleri ssp. scouleri

  • corolla pale purple to red purple
  • calyx tube clearly with 10 veins
  • lower leaves 12–20 mm wide
  • upper leaves 3–15 mm wide
  • Jepson claims the styles are about as long as the petal claws, but CalPhotos shows them often exserted
  • uncommon

san francisco campion
Silene verecunda

  • corolla (very) pale purple to reddish purple
  • calyx tube clearly with 10 veins
  • lower leaves 2–9 mm wide
  • upper leaves 2–6 mm wide
  • styles exserted
  • common
