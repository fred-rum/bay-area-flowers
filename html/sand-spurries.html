sand-spurries

Spergularia spp.
pink family (family Caryophyllaceae)

There are other sand spurries in the bay area, but I'm hesitant to include them when the differences are so inconspicuous.

red sand spurrey
Spergularia rubra
red sand spurrey
Spergularia rubra
  • annual, delicate
  • lower main stem 0.3–0.5 mm diam.
  • 2–4+ leaves per axillary cluster

sticky sand-spurrey
Spergularia macrotheca
sticky sand-spurrey
Spergularia macrotheca
  • strongly perennial herb, stout
  • lower main stem 0.8–3 mm diam.
  • 0–2+ leaves per axillary cluster
  • variants can be distinguished by flower color and style length (measure precisely!)
