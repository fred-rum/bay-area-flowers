two-color rabbit tobacco

Pseudognaphalium biolettii
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to rabbit-tobaccos (Pseudognaphalium spp.)

CalPhotos
  • Stem not glandular, sometimes ± woody near base, proximally tomentose to glabrous, but always tomentose near heads
  • Leaves not crowded
  • Leaf base clasping, not decurrent
  • adaxial leaf face ± bright green, tomentose or not, densely stalked-glandular
  • abaxial leaf face generally white-tomentose
  • Leaf flat or margin slightly curled under, often wavy
  • Inflorescence ± flat-topped
  • rare
