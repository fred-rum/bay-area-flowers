wintergreens

Pyrola spp.
Member of heather family (family Ericaceae)
flowering plants (subphylum Angiospermae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

 leafless wintergreen
Pyrola aphylla
leafless wintergreen
Pyrola aphylla
  • leaf blades absent or ≤ ± 1 cm, ± not green
  • rare

white-veined wintergreen
Pyrola picta

  • flower bract ≤ pedicel
  • leaf blades generally present, 2.5–7 cm, dark green with white mottling on veins adaxially
  • rare

bog wintergreen
Pyrola asarifolia

  • flower bract ≫ pedicel
  • leaf blades well developed, adaxially ± shiny
  • extremely rare; Big Basin
