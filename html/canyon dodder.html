canyon dodder

Cuscuta subinclusa
bindweed family (family Convolvulaceae)

Key to dodders (Cuscuta spp.)

  • corolla scales present
  • flower 5–7(9) mm
  • calyx and corolla lobes acute
  • calyx generally 1/2 corolla tube, lobe bases overlapped
  • corolla lobes 1/4–1/3 tube
  • anthers 0.8–2 mm
  • scattered inland

Chris’s observations: none
