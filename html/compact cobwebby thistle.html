compact cobwebby thistle

Cirsium occidentale var. compactum
Member of sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to cobwebby thistle (Cirsium occidentale)

  • heads short-peduncled, closely subtended by basal and large cauline leaves
  • phyllaries are significantly interconnected by cobwebby hairs
  • corolla dark rose-purple
  • very rare near San Bruno Mountain
