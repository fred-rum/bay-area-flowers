short-flower deervetch

Acmispon parviflorus
Member of legume family (family Fabaceae)
pink, cream in flowering plants

Key to deervetch (Acmispon spp.)

  • stem ascending to erect, 5–40 cm
  • 3–5 leaflets, 4–12 mm, elliptic to obovate, ± equal
  • peduncle generally > 1 cm, generally bracted
  • 1 flower per leaf axil
  • calyx lobes ± ≤ tube
  • corolla 4–6 mm, pink or salmon, quickly fading, wings ± = keel
  • common
