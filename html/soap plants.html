soap plants

Chlorogalum spp.
asparagus family (family Asparagaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

flowers open in evening

wavy-leafed soap plant
Chlorogalum pomeridianum
wavy-leafed soap plant
Chlorogalum pomeridianum
  • leaf 4–25 mm wide, margin generally wavy
  • common

narrowleaf soap plant
Chlorogalum angustifolium

  • leaf 2–5 mm wide, margin ± flat
  • very rare

flowers open during day

small-flowered soaproot
Chlorogalum parviflorum

  • perianth white or ± pink
  • flowers, buds 2–several per node
  • very rare

purple amole
Chlorogalum purpureum

  • perianth deep blue to purple
  • flowers, buds 1 per node
  • very rare
