california poppy

Eschscholzia californica
Member of poppy family (family Papaveraceae)

Key to Eschscholzia spp.

This species has subspecies or variants that don't seem worth distinguishing.

Some sources divide the species into two subspecies. The main subspecies is found throughout California. The “mexicana” subspecies is found only in the Sonoran Desert in southern California.
