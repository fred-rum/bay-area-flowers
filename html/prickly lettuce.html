prickly lettuce

Lactuca serriola
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

Key to lettuces (Lactuca spp.)

  • basal leaves generally 0 at flower
  • cauline leaves with narrow, acute, basal lobes clasping stem
  • leaves oblanceolate to oblong-elliptic or obovate in outline, margins prickly-toothed, sometimes also coarsely lobed
  • inflorescence branches often widely spreading
