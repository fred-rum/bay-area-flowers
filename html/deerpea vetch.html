deerpea vetch

Vicia ludoviciana ssp. ludoviciana
legume family (family Fabaceae)

Key to vetches (Vicia spp.)

There are no other members of this species in California.

  • 1–3 flowers, near tip, generally crowded
  • 4–10 leaflets, 10–25 mm, narrowly oblong to elliptic
  • leaflet tip acute, 1-toothed, or occasionally truncate
  • corolla 4.5–7 mm, pale blue [or deep blue] [or purple?]
  • stem sprawling or low-climbing, 30–100 cm
