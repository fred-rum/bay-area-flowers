field bindweed

Convolvulus arvensis

Key to bindweed family (family Convolvulaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

This species has no subspecies or variants.

  • stigma lobes linear to narrowly spoon-shaped, distinctly separated, tips acute [example]
  • calyx ± 5 mm
  • corolla ≤ 40 mm
  • anthers are often lined with pale-purple
  • bracts > 1 mm below calyx, not hiding it
