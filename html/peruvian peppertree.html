peruvian peppertree

Member of cashew family (family Anacardiaceae)

Key to peppertrees (Schinus spp.)

  • distal branches & twigs flexible, drooping
  • leaflets generally > 15, generally < 1 cm wide
  • rare; mostly in developed areas
