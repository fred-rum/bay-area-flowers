baby blue eyes genus

Nemophila spp.

baby blue eyes (Nemophila menziesii) - common throughout the bay area.

white nemophila (Nemophila heterophylla) - scattered throughout the bay area.

small flowered nemophila (Nemophila parviflora) - scattered throughout the bay area.

meadow nemophila (Nemophila pedunculata) - rare in the bay area.

eastwood's nemophila (Nemophila pulchella) - extremely rare in the bay area.
