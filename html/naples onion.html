naples onion

Allium neapolitanum
Member of amaryllis family (family Amaryllidaceae)

Key to onions (Allium spp.)

  • leaf 2–3, ≤ stem, flat
  • infloresence not ± 1-sided
  • pedicels 15–35 mm
  • flowers 7–12 mm, erect
  • perianth parts ± spreading, entire
  • ovary crest 0
  • in developed areas
