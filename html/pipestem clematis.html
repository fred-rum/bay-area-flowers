pipestem clematis

Clematis lasiantha
Member of buttercup family (family Ranunculaceae)
other colors in flowering plants

Key to Clematis spp.

  • leaflets generally 3–5
  • inflorescence generally 1-flowered
  • sepals 10–21 mm, ≫ stamens, hairy both abaxially and adaxially
  • flower Jan–Jun
  • Ecology: Hillsides, chaparral, open woodland
  • common

See also creamcups (Platystemon californicus).
