water primrose

Ludwigia hexapetala
evening primrose family (family Onagraceae)

Key to primose-willows (Ludwigia spp.)

  • bracts lanceolate to lance-ovate
  • sepals 8–19 mm
  • petals 15–29 mm
  • Despite the Latin name, typically has only 5 petals.
