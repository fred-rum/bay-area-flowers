marigolds

Calendula spp.
Member of yellow daisies
sunflower family (family Asteraceae)

There are no other species of this genus in California.

 field marigold
Calendula arvensis
field marigold
Calendula arvensis
  • head, including rays, generally 1–4 cm diam
  • 5–20 ray flowers, 5–8 mm
  • leaf ± lanceolate

pot marigold
Calendula officinalis

  • head, including rays, generally > 5 cm diam
  • 30–50+ ray flowers, generally 12–20 mm
  • leaf oblong to obovate
