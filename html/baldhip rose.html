baldhip rose

Rosa gymnocarpa
rose family (family Rosaceae)

Key to roses (Rosa spp.)

  • loose shrub
  • prickles 2–8 mm, ± slender, straight
  • leaf axis generally glabrous
  • leaflets glabrous
  • sepals glandular or not, entire
  • petals ± 10 mm, pink to red
  • very common in the bay area
