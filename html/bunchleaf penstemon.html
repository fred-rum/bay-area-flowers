bunchleaf penstemon

Penstemon heterophyllus
Member of plantain family (family Plantaginaceae)

Key to beardtongues (Penstemon spp.)

  • anther sacs 2.2–3 mm, dehiscing at proximal end to 1/2–2/3 their length, inner margins long-hairy
    • for all others, anther sacs dehiscing full length, generally spreading widely apart; anthers glabrous
  • inflorescence ± glabrous
  • corolla 23–40 mm
  • staminode glabrous
  • common
