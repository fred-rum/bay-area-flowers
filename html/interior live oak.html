interior live oak

Quercus wislizeni
beach family (family Fagaceae)

Key to oaks (Quercus spp.)

  • leaf:
    • 2–5 cm, roundish or lanceolate
    • adaxially ± flat
    • abaxially generally ± shiny, yellow-green
    • tip generally abruptly pointed
    • margin entire to spine-toothed
  • nut distally acute to ± obtuse
  • common
