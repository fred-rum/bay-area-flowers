valley mayweed
Matricaria occidentalis

  • plant 15–45(70) cm
  • peduncle ≤ 6.5 cm
  • undisturbed wet places
  • only in historical records

german chamomile
Matricaria chamomilla

  • daisy like, looks much like chamomiles (Anthemis spp.)
  • leaf lobes longer than for "real" chamomiles
  • very rare
