common hareleaf

Lagophylla ramosissima

Key to tarweeds and allies (tribe Madieae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

  • similar to grassy tarweed (Madia gracilis), but...
  • glandless stem
  • 2-5 calyx-like bracts subtending involucre
  • consistently 6 disk flowers (5 surrounding 1) and 5 ray flowers

Chris’s observations: none
