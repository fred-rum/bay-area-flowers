alumroots

Heuchera spp.
Member of saxifrage family (family Saxifragaceae)

Except for historical records that I'm ignoring, there are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

 crevice alumroot
Heuchera micrantha
crevice alumroot
Heuchera micrantha
  • hypanthium + calyx 1.5–3 mm
  • leaf blade 2–10 cm wide
  • common

island alumroot
Heuchera maxima

  • hypanthium + calyx 3.5–4.5 mm
  • leaf blade 6–18 cm wide
  • extremely rare at Coal Creek
