chamise

Adenostoma fasciculatum
Member of rose family (family Rosaceae)
white in flowering plants

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

This species has subspecies or variants that don't seem worth distinguishing.

The only bay-area subspecies is ssp. fasciculatum:

  • plant tall with erect/ascending branches
  • twigs glabrous
  • leaves 5–13 mm long

All other California species and subspecies are restricted to Southern California.
