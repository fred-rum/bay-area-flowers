pacific hound’s tongue

Adelinia grande
Member of borage family (family Boraginaceae)
blue in flowering plants

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

CalFlora has Cynoglossum occidentale in a few state parks in 2006, which may or may not be a misidentification. In any case, Cynoglossum is now split into other genuses, so C. occidentale is no longer grouped with C. grande.
