coralroots

Corallorhiza spp.
orchid family (family Orchidaceae)

There are no other species of this genus in the bay area.

striped coralroot
Corallorhiza striata
striped coralroot
Corallorhiza striata
  • 3-5 stripes
  • lip entire
spotted coralroot
Corallorhiza maculata
spotted coralroot
Corallorhiza maculata
  • no stripes
  • lip with 2 lobes laterally; lobes are rounded, prominent

Spotted coralroot has two varieties: var. maculata and var. occidentalis.

pacific coralroot
Corallorhiza mertensiana

  • no stripes
  • lip with 2 lobes laterally; lobes are acute, prominent or not
  • very rare
