{pinna,pinnae} The 1st-order division of a frond blade.
{pinnule,pinnules} The 2nd-order division of a frond blade.  (The 1st-order division of a pinna.)
{stipe,stipes} In ferns, the frond stalk; equivalent to petiole.  Compare to stipule.
